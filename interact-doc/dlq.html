Why messages end up here?

  • MaxDeliveryCountExceeded: The message was tried 10 times (default) and failed every time.
  • TTLExpiredException: The message sat in the queue longer than its Time-To-Live.
  • ManualDeadLetter: Your code explicitly called DeadLetterAsync().

Key Characteristics

  • Automatic: Every Queue/Subscription has one.
  • Path: queue-name/$DeadLetterQueue
  • Observation: You can't "send" to a DLQ directly.
